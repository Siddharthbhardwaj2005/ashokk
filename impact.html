Impact of Shri Asoke K. Ghosh

  • Empowered Indian students with affordable, world-class books, especially benefiting economically disadvantaged sections.
  • Revolutionized Indian publishing and elevated its global presence by bringing IPA Congress to New Delhi in 1992 and 2018.
  • Played a key role in Delhi being declared the UNESCO World Book Capital in 2003.
  • Significantly influenced higher education in India and abroad, with IITs and IIMs designing syllabi based on his publications.
  • His books are widely recommended in the 2018 AICTE syllabus and for competitive exams like Civil Services.
  • Encouraged eminent professors and scientists to author books, gaining them international recognition.
  • Publications across various disciplines have shaped student learning modules, with IBM collaborating to convert learning materials into textbooks.
  • Actively contributed to polio eradication efforts, aiding India's WHO-declared ‘Polio-free’ status.
